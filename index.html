FAQ

Common questions.

If something isn't answered here, send us a message at info@clustervise.com and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.

What does ClusterVise generate for each project?+

ClusterVise generates a structured BOM, stationwise I/O lists, supporting project documentation, and supplier-aware device selections based on the machine description you provide. Outputs expand by plan, with more workflow automation and integrations available in the higher tiers.

Which component brands and suppliers are in the catalogue?+

The catalogue covers 20,000+ components from leading European and international manufacturers across protection, control, automation, and enclosure categories. The full brand list is available on request — get in touch and we'll send the current supplier coverage for your region and machine types.

Can our engineers override or manually select components?+

Yes — always, at every step. Designers can browse the full 20,000+ component catalogue, filter by specification or brand, and pick any component manually. The AI's suggestions are exactly that: suggestions. Every manual selection or override also feeds back into the learning model, so it improves with your team's preferences over time.

Does ClusterVise integrate with CAD or ERP systems?+

The Professional plan includes ERP system integration. The Enterprise plan adds CAD integration and a full REST API for custom workflows and pipeline automation. If you have a specific integration requirement — Eplan, AutoCAD Electrical, SAP, or similar — mention it in your enquiry and we'll confirm current or planned support.

What happens when a component is out of stock or discontinued?+

ClusterVise uses live supplier data, so stock levels and availability are checked at the moment of design — not from a cached catalogue. When a preferred component is unavailable or discontinued, the platform automatically suggests pre-validated alternatives at a comparable specification and price point, so your design stays unblocked.

How does the AI learn our company's preferences?+

Every project your team runs builds the model's understanding of your organisation — preferred brands, trusted component families, and typical machine configurations. No manual curation is needed. Designer overrides and manual selections are also captured automatically. The more projects you run, the more precisely the platform reflects how your team works.

How long does onboarding take?+

After your enquiry, we'll arrange a live walkthrough for your team — typically within 48 hours. Most engineers produce their first AI-generated BOM during the walkthrough session itself. There's no lengthy implementation process: the platform works from plain-language input on day one, and becomes more tailored to your workflows as the team uses it.

We're still evaluating — can we see the platform in action first?+

Absolutely. Send us a message via the contact form with a brief description of the kind of machines you build and we'll set up a live demo tailored to your use case. No commitment required. We're currently in a guided pilot phase, so every new team gets hands-on support from the ClusterVise team through the evaluation process.
