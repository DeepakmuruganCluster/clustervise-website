Training Module

Training that actually covers the job.

Generic training platforms aren't built for engineering. They don't handle version-specific procedures, standards-based content, or the kind of judgment calls that take years to develop on the job.

See how it works
How it works

Technical training your team will actually use.

The Clustervise Training Module lets you build technical courses for your team. Create your own modules around your specific workflows, pull from Clustervise's pre-built content on BOM management and change control, or mix both. Difficulty is configurable per role or per person.

There's also a built-in AI assistant that answers questions mid-module, in context — so when an engineer hits a procedure they don't understand, they get an answer tied to the specific content they're looking at, not a generic search result.

Training materials are version-controlled. When a procedure changes, the training updates with it — and engineers who completed the old version are automatically flagged for a refresh. No manual tracking of who has read what.

Features

Built for the way engineering teams actually learn.

🏗️

Custom module builder

Build courses around your own workflows, tools, and standards. Drag-and-drop content blocks, embedded diagrams, and step-by-step procedure templates included.

📚

Pre-built engineering content

Pull from Clustervise's library of ready-made modules on BOM management, change control, component selection, and machine design fundamentals.

🎯

Role-configurable difficulty

Set module depth and required completion by role. A junior engineer and a senior designer see the same content at different levels of detail and required competency.

🤖

In-context AI assistant

Engineers can ask questions while working through a module. The AI assistant answers in the context of the specific content on screen — not a general knowledge search.
